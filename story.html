A fully-immersive story experience

A land divided

The city-state of Barrendrake has experienced turmoil for the last 300 years. War, poverty, and famine divide the country into three distinct political factions. Choose a side and decide what the fate of Barrendrake will be. You... and every other player who will be deciding to join your faction... or deny your faction.

A home destroyed

Barrendrake is a destitute, impoverished city-state. Your home, Fandale, was destroyed by the Black Army; How will you avenge your home, your family, your friends? You decide.

A villain unlike any other

Banners of Barrendrake has a unique villain: the player. Each season, players can apply to be the season's villain. Each villain is a General in the Black Army, and has at their disposal a multitude of weapons, soldiers, and funds to allocate every season. Will you be the one to unite Barrendrake under the banner of the Black Army? Or will you be defeated?
